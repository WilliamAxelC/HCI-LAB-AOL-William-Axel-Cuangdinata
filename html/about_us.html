About Us

Welcome to JCC, your premier conference center destination. We take pride in providing exceptional facilities and services for conferences, meetings, and corporate events. With our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced team, we aim to create the perfect environment for productive and memorable gatherings.

Our History

JCC was established in 1996, driven by the vision to create a space that fosters knowledge exchange, collaboration, and innovation. Over the years, we have hosted numerous successful conferences, seminars, workshops, and corporate events, earning the trust and recognition of our clients.

Our Mission

At JCC, our mission is to be the leading conference center, offering unmatched facilities and services to our clients. We strive to exceed expectations, foster innovation, and create an atmosphere conducive to learning, collaboration, and success.

Facilities

  • Modern Conference Rooms:
    Our conference center features spacious and well-equipped conference rooms, equipped with advanced audiovisual technology, comfortable seating, and professional ambiance.
  • Exhibition Halls:
    We have expansive exhibition halls, perfect for trade shows, product displays, and large-scale events. Our halls provide ample space for exhibitors to showcase their offerings effectively.
  • Breakout Rooms:
    For smaller group sessions and workshops, we offer comfortable breakout rooms designed to facilitate focused discussions and collaborative activities.
  • Business Lounges:
    Our business lounges provide a relaxed and professional setting for networking, informal meetings, and socializing during conferences.

Why Choose Us

  • Prime Location:
    Our conference center is strategically located, offering easy accessibility for attendees, with ample parking facilities and proximity to public transportation.
  • Exceptional Facilities:

    We understand that every conference is unique. We offer flexible packages and customizable options to meet your specific requirements, ensuring a tailored experience that aligns with your goals and objectives.
  • Commitment to Excellence:
