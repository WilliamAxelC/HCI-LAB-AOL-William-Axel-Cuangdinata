Who are we?

Discover Jockey's Convention Center, strategically nestled in the heart of Jakarta. For over 30 years, we have been the preferred destination for world-class events, welcoming distinguished political figures, influential business leaders, and renowned entertainers. With a rich portfolio of over 10,000 successful engagements, our experienced staff stands ready to fulfill your unique requirements.

Our versatile venue has witnessed a wide array of events, including global conventions, regional conferences, captivating theatrical performances, vibrant jazz festivals, sensational concerts, glamorous fashion shows, product launches, impactful business meetings, and elegant banquets. From the grandest gatherings to intimate affairs, we have the expertise to create memorable experiences across various industries.

Under one roof, you'll find state-of-the-art facilities that include expansive exhibition spaces, versatile event venues, and well-equipped meeting rooms. Immerse yourself in an environment enhanced by cutting-edge sound systems, captivating lighting setups, and seamless communication infrastructure. Our commitment to technological excellence ensures your event is delivered with precision and impact.

At Jockey's Convention Center, we understand that successful events require meticulous planning and execution. That's why our dedicated Convention and Exhibition Services Managers are on hand to guide you through every step of the process. From conceptualization to realization, our team will work closely with you to bring your vision to life. With attention to detail and a focus on personalized service, we take care of all the logistics, including flower arrangements, lighting management, and contingency planning.

Choose Jockey's Convention Center for your next event and experience unparalleled service, world-class facilities, and a commitment to excellence. Join the ranks of presidents, CEOs, international musicians, and industry-leading companies who have trusted us to deliver exceptional events. Let us create a memorable occasion that exceeds your expectations and leaves a lasting impression on your guests.
